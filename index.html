Exam Syllabus and Study Guide

Mathematics

What to Study

  • Number System, Decimals, Fractions
  • LCM, HCF, Ratio & Proportions, Percentage
  • Time & Work, Time & Distance, Mensuration
  • Simple & Compound Interest, Profit & Loss
  • Geometry, Trigonometry Basics, Statistics

Where to Study

  • NCERT Maths Books (Class 6-10)
  • RS Aggarwal's Quantitative Aptitude
  • Lucent's General Mathematics

General Intelligence and Reasoning

What to Study

  • Analogies, Coding-Decoding, Puzzles, Syllogism
  • Data Sufficiency, Series Completion, Venn Diagrams
  • Statement-Conclusion, Decision Making

Where to Study

  • RS Aggarwal’s Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning
  • Lucent's General Intelligence and Reasoning
  • Practice Online Mock Tests

General Awareness

What to Study

  • Current Affairs (National & International)
  • Indian History & Freedom Struggle
  • Geography: Physical, Social, Economic
  • Indian Polity, Constitution, Governance
  • General Science (up to 10th level)

Where to Study

  • Lucent’s General Knowledge
  • NCERT Science Books (Class 6-10)
  • Daily Newspaper (The Hindu, Indian Express)
